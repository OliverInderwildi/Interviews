Quantum Leap

The staggering possibilities and grave dangers of quantum technologies — as quantum and AI reshape the foundations of industry, security, and society.

About the Interview

In this feature for ADVANTAGE Magazine, Managing Editor Margaux Daubry explores the quantum technology revolution with Dr. Oliver Inderwildi as one of the key voices. The piece examines how quantum computing, sensing, and communication are moving from niche research toward commercial reality — and what that shift means for industry, geopolitics, and society.

Inderwildi discusses the symbiotic convergence of quantum computing and AI, the practical hurdles that remain, and the urgent cybersecurity threat of "harvest now, decrypt later" strategies. The interview culminates in a philosophical question about whether humanity can embed an ethical compass in these systems — and how long we will remain in control.

"The big philosophical question is whether we can give it an ethical and moral compass and, if so, which ethical and moral compass should it be?"
